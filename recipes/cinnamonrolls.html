TJ's GF Cinnamon Rolls

Ingredients

  • TJ's GF pizza dough
  • brown sugar
  • cinnamon
  • butter
  • vanilla extract
  • cream cheese
  • powdered sugar

Instructions

  1. follow instructions on TJ's GF dough - needs to rest for 30 min

    2. in mean time:

  2. melt some butter (NOT ALL)
  3. mix melted butter, brown sugar, cinnamon, and splash of vanilla in bowl
  4. in different bowl (preferably mixer), mix cream cheese,butter, powdered sugar, & vanilla

    5. back to dough:

  5. roll out dough
  6. spread sugar mixture on dough
  7. roll up dough
  8. cut into rolls
  9. place in baking dish & follow baking instructions from dough paacket
  10. after baking, spread cream cheese mixture on top
  11. enjoy! (:
